Once Your Inside

  • Before being inside, you may here some happy music, that music would be “Somewhere Over the Rainbow/It’s a Wonderful World” by Israel “IZ” Kamakawiwo’ole. I do this so that you know that it is a positive, safe environment.
  • On the whiteboard my room number, my name, and the room number, so you know you are in the right classroom.
  • One every desk there will be a folded card with a number on it, say hello to your new seat. So you don't have to wait and wonder "Do I have a friend in this class?" You can just go to your seat!

    • The Greetings I Give

  • Expect a large smile from me for I am thrilled to have you in my classroom.
  • Expect me to shake your hand, call you by name, and tell you "Welcome to Mr. Burr's (enter subject) class, room (room number). It is such a pleasure to have you. Also if you happened to find the wrong room at least you know before you enter. It was a pleasure to meet you.
  • You will be handed a piece of paper with a number on it. This will be your assigned seat.
  • If I notice that you enter the room loudly or go to the wrong seat I will come up to you and kindly ask you to come back up to the door and say, "Please walk back inside quietly and go to the seat with your number on it." I only do this because some students may get flustered by how loud you are. I want everyone to feel safe and relaxed in my class.

    • The Introduction: DUH DUH DUUUUUUH

  • When I introduce myself to you, I will be standing in front of everyone, not behind a table or a desk, but to where everyone can see me so that way you know that I am open, I am not here for you.
  • I will constantly be smiling in front of you so that way you know that I am excited to have you here.
  • When I tell you of my expectations for each and every one of you, they will be positive. I want you to feel safe and welcomed inside of this class.
  • To help me explain my expectations I will have a power point prepared to go through with you. There will also be hand motions so that way you aren't just listening to me droaning on but you can get engaged in them too
  • I will also go through how I will like to manage my classroom. We will even practice these methods I have prepared for you!
  • Again for these expect a power point and some hand motions to help you remember them.

    • Jobs: What!? MADNESS!

  • Inside of the classroom there will be jobs. Every student will have a job that they will be responsible for doing throughout class.
  • Every desk also has a cost of rent that you will pay with the money you make from your job
  • For more information check out the jobs page
