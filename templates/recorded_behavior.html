Positive Behavior Recording

  • As you do great things, like I know will do quite often, I will record it in my positive behavior binder which will have a huge smiley face on it to signify positive behavior. This binder will be separated into a few different sections (ex: phone call, note home, prize ticket, etc.)
  • Whenever you do something great I will try my best to always write a note called the "You are durn awesome" to let you know what an amazing job you have done.

    • Negative Behavior Recording

  • Just like the positive binder I will also have a negative binder that will have a huge sad face on it. This will have sections like tardiness, missing homework, misbehavior, etc.
  • On the table next to the door there will be a sheet that says "tardy" that will ask for name, date, time, and signature. If you are ever tardy I will ask you to fill this sheet out.
  • If you ever have missing homework I will have a missing homework sheet for you to fill out. You will need to fill out your name, homework missing, date, why you are missing it, and sign it.
  • If you do anything that is disruptive in class, or not following the guidelines you will fill out a "wha' ha-happened was" form that will have your name and what you did already filled and then will ask you why you did what you did, a way we can change the behavior, and for your signature.
