There will always be some kind of quote from the theme that the class and I choose from that displays that the classroom is an open positive environment.

Most of the themes we will be using will have characters that display unconditional affection towards their friends and they will be posted at the top of the door to remind the you what you should strive for.

Bilbo also is a symbol for going out of your comfort zone, which in his case was to go on an adventure. Life is an adventure and sometimes you have to go out of your comfort zone.

The door will change at least once a month to two months. You, the other students, and I will decide what the doors theme should be that month to two months. We will try out best to strive for themes that inspire us to do positive things. Whether it is something that has to do with going back to the earth (gardening), or a movie or a book that has inspired greatness in us.
