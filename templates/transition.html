Lunch Transition

  • Put away your materials.
  • Grab your lunch box/bag/money if you have it.
  • Line up in front of the door.
  • Wait for me to say go ahead.
  • Once I tell you to go ahead you will walk down the hallway respectfully to other classes toward the lunch room.
  • I will lock the door behind you all so that way you know your things are safe.

    • After Lunch Transition

  • I will take you back to the classroom once you are lined up in the cafeteria and your tables are clean.
  • I will unlock the door so you can get our stuff so that you can go to your next class.

    • Assembly Transition

  • Put materials inside of backpack.
  • Grab your backpack.
  • Line up in front of the door.
  • Wait for me to tell you to go ahead.
  • I will lock the door behind us.

    • Emergency Procedures

  • No matter what I will always grab my student roster so that I know each of your lovely faces are safe.
  • I will always guide you through what to do for the emergency procedures in a calm manner. Whether it is walk to our destination during a fire drill, or to curl up in a ball during a tornado drill.
  • I will stay calm so that way you know that I am prepared and am ready for what is going on.
