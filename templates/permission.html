Need to Go? Sign it!

If you need to use the restroom you are going to make the sign for bathroom. To do this you will place your thumb between your pointer and middle finger and then move our wrist left and right. You can go when I sign yes.

Need Some Water? Sign it!

If you need to get a drink of water, it will be the same as bathroom, you are going to sign it. You do this by making a "W" with your pointer, middle, and ring finger then move it to and from your mouth. You can go when I sign yes.

Need to Sharpen a Dull Pencil? Raise it!

If you need to sharpen your pencil, raise your pencil in the air along with your other hand. This way I can just look at you and know "Hey, you need to sharpen, go nuts." You can go when I sign yes.
How to Know You Can or Can't Go
To signal you that you can or can't go I will sign you either yes
or no.

Why I Use Sign?

I use sign language so that way I can give you a quick yes or no answer for these things. I won't have to walk across the room and you won't have to wait for me to walk across.
