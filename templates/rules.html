Rule 1: Respect Everyone

  • We will respect everyone because nobody wants to be disrespected. Nobody wants to get told anything hurtful.
  • We will practice this every day. Every period we will look left and right and give our neighbors a compliment.

    • Rule 2: Be Responsible

  • We have to be responsible for our actions. Whether we are late, forget to do an assignment, or even if we talk when we shouldn't be talking. In a job we are responsible for our actions. As a students you are responsible for yours.
  • This will be practiced every day. Whether it is with classwork, homework, by being present, etc.

    • Rule 3: Do Them Motions to Get Up

  • We have procedures for a reason, they keep things running smoothly inside of the classroom.
  • As we get to a permission we will practice the motion.
  • Students will use motions daily.

    • Rule 4: Make Smart Choices

  • Every day we have to make choices, do I want pizza or donuts, water or soda? Sometimes though we can make choices that matter that can be harmful or helpful to you or someone else. Think through your options and choose which one seems most beneficial without any emotions persuading you.
  • We will sometimes have a brain sweat that will have a scenario where the student has to make a decision.
