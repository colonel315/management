What are Centers?

Centers are an interactive way for you to learn pieces of information on a particular topic and normally have learning materials to help enhance the education a you will receive.

Center Rotations

You will rotate in a clockwise pattern, this way it is consistent and as little confusion as possible for you. I do this just so that way every transition can be as smooth as possible.

How Long Will I Be in a Center?

Since we have about 75 minutes per class we will have about 24 minutes per center so that way we have about 5 minutes left at the end of class to clean up and do our exit slip.

How Do I Know Where to Go Next?

So now you know how long you will be there, the rotation pattern of centers, well what if you forget? Well let us just say there will be arrows on the ground to remind you what direction to go. Also on each desk there will be a table that tells you the order to go.
