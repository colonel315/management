As you walk in you will notice that my desk is organized and ready for that day and for new paperwork to come in.
As you walk in, the desks will be prepared for the day, if there are materials needed for the days activities it will already be set up on the tables.
The bulletin board will be prepared by having that months activities, if there are any field trips the dates of them, and some motivational quotes so that way you can just feel good about yourself.
You will notice a clean filing cabinet that is alphabetized for each student.
Here you will find manipulatives for you to work with whenever we have recreation time so that way you can work on other topics that you may be struggling in, or you may just want to get a refresher in. These will be organized by topic, then alphabetized so that way it is easy to find.
Every day you walk in these folders will be empty, so that way you know that I've already taken up that days work and started to grade it.
The guide-lines will stay up the entire school year, but the motivational posters will periodically change throughout the school year so that way you can get motivated for different things. I will try my best to keep the posters relavent to what we are learning.
The paper work tray will be empty and filed every day during lunch or after school so that way only I, or the office, have the capability to see what the information is.
The rotation cards will laminated, and replaced whenever they need to be. This way they can stay legible, and also so that way you can feel like you are in a clean environment.
These books will be organized by fiction or non-fiction and then by maturity level so that way you have easy access to the books that you are allowed to read and you don't have to keep questioning whether or not the book is appropriate for you.
The door will be decorated so that way you can feel invited to the classroom every day. I will be sure to keep the door positive and, possibly, motivational.
The white board will be wiped clean every class that they are used by our lovely janitors, so that way they are ready for the next class.
The student area will be clean and ready for students to go and learn at with the manipulatives at the manipulative shelf.
The reading area will be clean, clear of any books that students may have been using, and ready for student to sit in some comfy chairs/bean bags and read a nice book.
