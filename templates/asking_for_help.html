Asking For Help With Independent Work

  • Each of you lovely students will have a trifold that says on one side "Need me some help!" and on the other it will say "Keep working." So when you need help you will face the "Need me some help! side towards me, so that the "Keep Working" side is facing towards you. This is so that while you wait for help, you are reminded to move on and keep working while you wait. No need to stop and get a lesser grade when you can get a higher one if you continue on.

    • Asking For Help When at Centers

  • Before you ask me, see if anybody else knows the answer.
  • One person can raise their hand and continue to work on the next question. No need for the whole group to wait for one question.

    • Asking For Help When Doing Cooperative Learning

  • Same as centers, before you ask, try asking one of your partners. They may know the answer.
  • One person can raise their hand and continue to work on the next question. No need for the whole group to wait for one question.
